🛠 Step‑by‑Step Instructions: Build a Properly Formatted Excel Table

Step 1: Set Up a Small Excel Table

  • Open a new Excel file.
  • Create a 5×3 table with columns: Year, Revenue Growth (%), and Total Revenue ($M).
  • Fill in years 2020 to 2024 as row headers.
  • Bold the column headers and row labels.

Step 2: Apply Light Gray Fill for Historical Data

  • Enter historical revenue numbers (e.g., 200, 215, 230 for 2020–2022).
  • Fill those cells with a light gray background.
  • Keep the font color black.

Step 3: Add Manual Inputs in Blue Font

  • In the Revenue Growth (%) column for 2023 and 2024, enter projected values (e.g., 6.0%, 7.0%).
  • Format those cells with blue font.

Step 4: Add Formulas in Black Font

  • Calculate 2023 Revenue using a formula like =C4*(1+B5).
  • Drag the formula down to 2024.
  • Leave font color black to signal these are formulas.
