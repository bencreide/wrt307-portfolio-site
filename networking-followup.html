Introduction

Following up turns a great chat into a lasting connection. Done right, it shows professionalism, memory, and intent.

Steps to Follow Up Properly

  1. Follow up within 24 hours
  2. Thank them and reference something specific
  3. Mention next steps if relevant
  4. Keep it short and warm
  5. Set a reminder to reconnect
