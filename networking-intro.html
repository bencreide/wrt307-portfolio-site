Introduction

A strong introduction sets the tone for every professional conversation. It reflects confidence, purpose, and respect for the person’s time.

Steps to Craft Your Introduction

  1. Start with who you are and what you’re studying
  2. Mention a relevant experience or goal
  3. Make a quick, specific connection to their work
  4. Close with a light conversational lead-in or question
  5. Optional: Add a friendly opener
