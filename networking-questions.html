Asking Quality Questions During a Conversation

← Back to Networking

Introduction

Strong questions show preparation and engagement. They deepen the conversation and demonstrate your curiosity and judgment.

Steps to Ask Better Questions

  1. Prep 2–3 focused questions
  2. Avoid yes/no or easily‑Googled questions
  3. Add context before asking
  4. Listen actively and follow up
  5. Don’t dominate the conversation
